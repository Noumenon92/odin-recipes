Hong Shao Rou

Description

Aromatic stewed pork in a thick spicy-sweet sauce.

Ingredients

  • 80ml of chinese cooking wine SHAO XING
  • 50ml of light soy sauce
  • 100g of crushed palm sugar
  • 4 crushed gloves of garlic
  • 1 chopped chilli pepper
  • 4 spring onions
  • 1 cinnamon
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 medium size thick cut ginger
  • 800mg thick cut pork

Steps

  1. Throw pork into a boiling water.
  2. Boil for 2 minutes.
  3. Rinse pork.
  4. Heat a frying pan and throw palm sugar on it.
  5. Wait until the sugar changes color to dark brown.
  6. Put the pork in a pan and stir until the meat is covered with caramel.
  7. Put garlic, chili, onions, cinnamon, and ginger in a pan.
  8. Pour soy sauce and wine.
  9. Cook for 10 minutes.
  10. After 10 minutes of cooking on the pan, replace the pan with a saucepan and put everything into it.
  11. Refill the saucepan with water and stew everything for 40 minutes.
  12. After 40 minutes, put down the cover of the saucepan and fry meat while stirring - do it until the sauce thickens.
  13. Serve it immediately with rice, or wait until tomorrow.
