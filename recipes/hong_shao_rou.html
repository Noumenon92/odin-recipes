Hong Shao Rou

Description

Aromatic stewed pork in a thick spicy-sweet sauce

Ingredients

  • 80ml of chinese cooking wine SHAO XING
  • 50ml of light soy sauce
  • 100g of crushed palm sugar
  • 4 crushed gloves of garlic
  • 1 chopped chilli pepper
  • 4 spring onions
  • 1 cinnamon
  • 2 star anise
  • 1 medium size thick cut ginger
  • 800mg thick cut pork

Steps

  1. throw pork into a boiling water
  2. boil for 2 minutes
  3. rinse pork
  4. heat frying pan and throw palm sugar on it
  5. wait until sugar changes color to dark brown
  6. put pork on pan and stir until meat is covered with caramel
  7. put garlic, chilli, onions, cinnamon and ginger on pan
  8. pour soy sauce and wine
  9. cook for 10 minutes
  10. after 10 minutes of cooking on the pan, replace pan with saucepan and put everything into it
  11. refill saucepan with water and stew everything for 40 minutes
  12. after 40 minutes put down cover of saucepan and fry meat while stiring - do it untill sauce thickens
  13. serve it immediately with rice or wait untill tomorrow
